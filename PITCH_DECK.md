# ZimHeritage — Pitch Deck

## Transforming Zimbabwean Education Through Technology

---

## The Problem

- **1.5M+ children** in Zimbabwe have limited access to quality learning resources
- **Curriculum-aligned content** is scarce outside expensive private schools
- **Home learning support** is virtually non-existent for most families
- **Past exam preparation** requires expensive extra lessons or purchase of materials
- **Teachers** lack digital tools for creating and managing assignments
- **Parents** have no visibility into their children's daily learning progress

## The Solution

**ZimHeritage**: A comprehensive, curriculum-aligned digital learning platform available on mobile devices.

### What We Offer

| Feature | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| Full Curriculum (ECD A → Form 6) | Covers every grade level in the Zimbabwe system |
| Interactive Lessons with Explanations | Makes home learning possible and effective |
| Past Exam Questions with Model Answers | Democratises exam preparation |
| AI-Personalised Tutoring | 24/7 learning support for every student |
| Homework & Submission System | Bridges the gap between teachers and students |
| Progress Analytics | Gives parents and teachers real-time insights |
| Offline-First Architecture | Works without internet — crucial for rural areas |
| Visual Aids for ECD-Grade 2 | Age-appropriate learning support for early learners |

## Market Opportunity

- **40M+ population** of Zimbabwe, 60% under 25
- **4.5M+ students** in primary and secondary education
- **Zimbabwe Heritage-Based Curriculum** (2024-2030) creates a national mandate for digital learning tools
- **Smartphone penetration** growing at 15%+ annually
- **Government digitalisation push** under National Development Strategy 1 (NDS1)

## Competitive Advantage

| Factor | ZimHeritage | Competitors |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Curriculum Alignment | Full ZIMSEC alignment | Mostly international (UK/US) curricula |
| Offline Capability | Built-in from day one | Internet required |
| AI Integration | Gemini-powered tutor | Basic or no AI |
| Multi-Role | Student, Teacher, Parent, Admin | Student-only |
| Cost | Freemium with free core content | Paid subscriptions |
| Local Relevance | Zimbabwe heritage focus | Generic content |

## Traction & Milestones

- **Phase 1 (Complete)**: Core app with curriculum data, lessons, and demo mode
- **Phase 2 (Current)**: Past exam questions, visual aids, enhanced AI tutoring
- **Phase 3 (Next)**: Full content library for all subjects/grades, teacher tools
- **Phase 4 (Future)**: Community features, marketplace, offline distribution via SD cards

## Business Model

1. **Freemium**: Free access to core curriculum content and basic features
2. **Premium**: Advanced AI features, detailed analytics, priority support
3. **Institutional**: School-wide licenses with teacher management dashboard
4. **Government**: Ministry-level deployment for national digital learning initiative
