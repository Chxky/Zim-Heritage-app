# App Store & Play Store Listing

## App Name
**ZimHeritage** — Zimbabwe Heritage Curriculum

## Subtitle
Learn the Zimbabwe Heritage-Based Curriculum on any device

## Description (Full)

ZimHeritage is a comprehensive learning platform for the Zimbabwe Heritage-Based Curriculum, covering ECD A through Form 6. Built in partnership with educators and aligned with the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education framework, ZimHeritage brings quality education to every Zimbabwean learner.

**For Students:**
- Full curriculum coverage across 15 grade levels
- Interactive lessons with clear explanations, examples, and practice questions
- Past exam questions (Grade 5 to Form 6) with model answers and marking guides
- AI-powered tutor for personalised learning assistance
- Progress tracking across all subjects and topics

**For Teachers:**
- Create and distribute homework assignments digitally
- Access answer keys and marking guides
- AI-assisted lesson planning and assessment design
- Review and provide feedback on student submissions

**For Parents:**
- Monitor your children's learning progress in real-time
- View homework assignments and assessment results
- Stay informed with school updates and notifications

**For Administrators:**
- Platform-wide analytics and usage metrics
- Curriculum overview across all grade levels
- User management and role assignment

**Key Features:**
- Complete Zimbabwe Heritage-Based Curriculum (ECD A - Form 6)
- 100+ lessons with study content written for home learning
- Past exam papers with detailed solutions
- Google Gemini AI Tutor for 24/7 personalised help
- Offline mode — works without internet
- Zimbabwe flag-inspired design
- Multi-language support (English, Shona, Ndebele)

**Subjects Covered:**
English, Mathematics, Shona/Ndebele, Heritage-Social Studies, General Science, Agriculture, Visual and Performing Arts, Family Religion and Moral Education, Physical Education, ICT/Computer Science, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, History, Geography, Economics, Accounting, Commerce, Business Studies, Literature, Divinity, and more.

**Grade Levels:**
ECD A, ECD B, Grade 1, Grade 2, Grade 3, Grade 4, Grade 5, Grade 6, Grade 7, Form 1, Form 2, Form 3, Form 4, Form 5 (Lower 6), Form 6 (Upper 6)
