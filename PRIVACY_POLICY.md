# ZimHeritage Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

## 1. Introduction

ZimHeritage ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name, email address, and password (for authentication)
- Role (student, teacher, parent, or admin)
- Grade level and school affiliation
- Age (for age-appropriate content delivery)

### Usage Data
- Learning progress and assessment scores
- Homework submissions and feedback
- Subject and topic preferences
- AI tutor interactions

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version and usage statistics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide personalised learning content aligned with the Zimbabwe Heritage-Based Curriculum
- To track educational progress and generate performance reports
- To enable AI-powered tutoring assistance via Google Gemini API
- To facilitate homework creation, submission, and marking
- To improve our educational content and user experience
- To comply with Zimbabwean educational regulations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored securely using Firebase infrastructure
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- We implement access controls to ensure data is only accessible to authorised users

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase** (Google): Authentication, Firestore database, and Storage
- **Google Gemini AI**: AI tutoring assistance
- **Google Fonts**: Typography

These services have their own privacy policies governing data handling.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

## 7. Children's Privacy

ZimHeritage is designed for learners of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with applicable laws regarding children's data:

- Parental consent is required for learners under 18
- Parents can access and monitor their children's learning data
- No personal information is sold or shared for marketing purposes

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your learning data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## 9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact:
- Email: privacy@zimheritage.education
- Address: ZimHeritage Education, Harare, Zimbabwe

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
